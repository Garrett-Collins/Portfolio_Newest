T-SQL Code In SSMS

Analysis On Superbowl QB's and Their College Qb Coaches

After web scraping 3 different data sources, I found that only 31.8% of the highest paid college qb coaches have produced a quarterback who has gone to an nfl superbowl. 84.4% of all Superbowl quarterbacks, were trained by a college qb coach, that was not in the top 22 highest paid college qb coaches. In conclusion, upcoming college quarterbacks that are most concerned with making it to an NFL superbowl in their future, should not base their college qb coach mentor, off of how much the coach is being paid.
